# 3D Printing Guitar Picks
Much like good socks, good guitar plectrums are great at what they do until they disappear and make you buy new ones, sadly an often-occuring inevitability. The ritual of watching each pick from a recently-opened fresh pack of your favorite type (which, of course, you swore to keep track of this time) vanish into couch cushions is an affliction which quietly distinguishes most guitar players from any other being and was the persistent nuisance that led to me begin 3d-printing guitar picks on an FDM printer two years ago, recently lost the file, so redisgening, designed mine as a normal jazz pick, 24mm by 28mm, import canvas, calibrate tool, dxf file of outline (https://vagran.github.io/dxf-viewer-example/) meow power slinky
